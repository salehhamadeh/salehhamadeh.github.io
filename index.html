I am a full-stack software developer based in New York. In the past year, I started my own company building MailWatcher, an email client that intelligently organizes and manages your mailbox.

Before starting my business, I worked at Scale AI's 2D team as a staff software engineer and engineering manager. My team built image and video annotation tools and worked directly with clients to help them create the datasets they need for ML training. Before that, I worked on Reserve with Google as part of Google Maps and on Redfin's search team.

I graduated from Georgia Tech with a degree in Computer Science with concentration in devices and modeling/simulation. While attending college, I interned at Yahoo! and BrainJocks as a frontend developer intern and a .NET developer intern, respectively.

When I'm free, I like to hike, write, work from coffee shops, and help small business get online.

If you have an interesting project for me or just want to talk, hit me up on email or LinkedIn.
