About me

If I were to choose one word to describe myself, I would choose "ambitious." Besides being a developer, programmer, hacker, blogger, starboxer*, and technology enthusiast, I am an overly optimistic person who is trying to keep pace with and eventually beat the amazingly fast rate at which technology is evolving. I am increasingly interested in the future of the web and where it is heading to with WebSockets, Big Data, and all the new technologies. Even though I failed in keeping pace with the open source community many times, I am still trying to get my head into the game. At the moment, I am learning about Meteor.JS, Node.JS, and Socket.IO, as well as tech startups and all their business stuff.

Other than creating things, I also enjoy reading, walking and jogging, tennis, and swimming. My favorite novel is Gone with the Wind by Margaret Mitchell, and my favorite history book is Code: The Hidden Language of Computer Hardware and Software by Charles Petzold.
